RCSM 2025

(Recent Trends in Computing and Smart Mobility Conference)

5-6 December 2025

MANIT Bhopal, India

IEEE Conference Number: 67767

RCSM-2025 is the 1st edition of the Recent Trends in Computing and Smart Mobility Conference. The conference series brings together researchers from computing and electrical backgrounds to meet Industry 4.0 standards and contribute to the Make in India mission.

This conference brings together academicians, researchers, Industry people, and students to discuss the current state-of-the-art developments of the relevant fields. The conference will provide a benchmark and platform to the "AATM NIRBHAR BHARAT".

All accepted and presented papers will be sent to IEEE Explore for publication if paper's meet the standards. Conference proceedings that meet IEEE quality review standards may be eligible for inclusion in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library. IEEE reserves the right not to publish any proceedings that do not meet these standards.
