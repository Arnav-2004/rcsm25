Call for Papers

RCSM-2025 is the 1st edition of the Recent Trends in Computing and Smart Mobility Conference. The conference series brings together researchers from computing and electrical backgrounds to meet Industry 4.0 standards and contribute to the Make in India mission.

Original contributions based on the results of research and development are solicited. Prospective authors are requested to submit their papers in standard IEEE conference format in not more than 6 pages, in the following broad areas (but not limited to). Authors must ensure the relevance of their submissions to the scope, name, and theme of the conference.

The topics covered (but are not limited to) in the conference are as follows:

  • AI, ML & Computational Techniques
  • Signal & Image Processing
  • Cloud & Big Data Technology
  • Quantum Computing
  • Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Robotics & Drone Technology
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Electrical Vehicle
  • Electric Device
  • Application Of Electronics and Electrical Engineering
